CXV services

Powerful features in one product package.

Knowledge integrity

CXV validates organisational knowledge integrity, rectifies the gaps and ensures every aspect of infrastructure configurations has been documented.

infrastructure resiliency

CXV keeps history of all assets used to build the infrastructure and constantly tracks them. It ensures all assets are available at all times and are easy to locate and access, in case there is a need to rebuild the infrastructure at any level.

Improve security posture

CXV improves the security posture of Enterprise IT to meet industry common standards (ISO2700x). It detects and alarms unauthorized changes and maintains a clear historical view of the activities by all authorized users.

risk management

CXV ensures all security risks are documented and well understood, and ensures the knowledge about the risks are up to date and in time of crisis are easy available.
